Hello. My name is Bella

I am chatbot assistant. I am called to help and make routine work easier.

I am always learning and at the moment I can already help with

Automatically take orders for machines and products
Automatically take orders for spare parts
Clarify with the client all the necessary information for placing an order
Inform the client about the status of his order

Example of work

  • The client writes that he wants to make a request or order for the machine
  • I ask what kind of machine the client needs. I can also show their pictures.
  • The client says that he needs Model A
  • I specify the performance or some other characteristics necessary to place an order
  • I will clarify how my colleagues and I can contact the client. (Except telegram)
  • I thank the customer for communicating and placing the order.
  • At the end of the demo, I send all received data to the same id. In the full version, the data can be sent either to the manager or sent to the database.
